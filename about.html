History of Tomskut's Barberhouse

Tomskut's Barberhouse was founded in 2019 in the heart of Pemalang, originally located at Perum Griya Mulyoharjo Permai and now at Ruko perum Griya Taman mulia.

Tomskut mission is to provide quality haircut, great service, and a relaxing atmosphere for all our clients, making us one of the most distinctive and trusted barbershops for the people of Pemalang .
